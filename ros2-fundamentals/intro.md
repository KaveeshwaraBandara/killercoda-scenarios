# ROS 2 Fundamentals — hands-on

This is a **real Linux machine** in your browser. In this scenario you'll practice the core skills of Stage 1 of the course:

- **inspect** a running ROS 2 graph — nodes, topics, types, rates
- **inject** your own messages into a topic from the command line
- **write** a publisher node of your own, in Python
- **write** a service server, and call it
- **tune** a running node from outside, without restarting it

ROS 2 Jazzy is installing in the background while you read — it'll be ready when you need it.

Part of the free course **[Learn ROS 2 from Zero](https://kaveeshwarabandara.github.io/ros2-course/)**. If terms like *node* and *topic* are new, do [Stage 0](https://kaveeshwarabandara.github.io/ros2-course/stage-0-first-win/run-ros2-in-your-browser/) first — it takes five minutes.
